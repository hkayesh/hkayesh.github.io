Hospitals run on predictions: how long will each patient stay, and how many will arrive tomorrow?
At HealthcareLogic I designed and implemented machine learning models for both questions.

- **Length-of-stay model** — re-engineered the existing prediction model, improving its accuracy
  by more than 20% and enabling more precise allocation of beds and clinical resources.
- **Arrival-rate model** — a patient arrival rate prediction model that improved operational
  efficiency and resource management.
- **Care planning** — predictions feed directly into patient care planning workflows, helping
  hospitals optimise for both outcomes and cost.
